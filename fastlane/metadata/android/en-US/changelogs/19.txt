The search got two bigger changes:

1. a new POI search feature. E.g. searching 'restaurants berlin' or zoom in and
search just 'restaurants'. Other words are: bank, atm, hotel, parking,
airport, bus stop, education, hospital, leisure, museum, parks, pharmacy,
playground, public transit, train, railway station, school, super market,
tourism, police, gas station, post, post box, charging, charging station,
shop, toilet, water.

2. the address search was improved and results in the current view are
preferred. And nominatim search is now triggered if 'Enter' is pressed -
there is no separate suggestion item anymore.

A new entry 'zoom to route' was added.
